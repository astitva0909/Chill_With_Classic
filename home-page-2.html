Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a classic North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a luscious, creamy tomato-based gravy. It's flavored with aromatic spices like garam masala, cumin, and coriander, and finished with a touch of butter for richness and flavor.
